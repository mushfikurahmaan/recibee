Introduction

Welcome to Recibee's Privacy Policy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app and website.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information (email, name, profile picture)
  • Usage data (recipes viewed, saved, created)
  • Device information (device type, operating system)
  • Photos you upload for recipe detection

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our recipe detection service
  • Personalize your recipe recommendations
  • Send you updates about new features
  • Analyze app usage to improve user experience

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. This includes:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits
  • Secure access controls
  • Regular backups

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request data correction
  • Delete your account
  • Export your data
